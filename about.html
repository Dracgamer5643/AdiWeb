About Me

General Info :

Hey there, I'm Aditya Patil, a Mumbai-born and raised tech enthusiast, currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Engineering. My journey into the world of technology started early, driven by a passion for Web-Development and a curious mind. Born on 12th Nov 2004, I've grown up immersed in the vibrant tech culture of Mumbai.Over the past few years, I've delved deep into coding, mastering languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Django, and Python.

Achievements/Experience :

I've been actively engaged in various coding competitions, including participating in the Smart India Hackathon (SIH), where I excelled and qualified for the final round. This experience has further honed my problem-solving skills and innovation in coding.Beyond competitions, I've leveraged my skills to satisfy the needs of more than 20 clients on freelancing platforms like Fiverr. Crafting over 10 successful websites has been a rewarding experience, focusing not only on aesthetics but also on delivering efficient functionalities that align with client visions. One of my proudest achievements includes developing an AI chatbot integrated with APIs and automation techniques in Python. This project demonstrates my commitment to pushing boundaries and exploring the possibilities of technology.
